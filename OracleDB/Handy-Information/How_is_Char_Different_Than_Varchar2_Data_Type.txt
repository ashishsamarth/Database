CHAR:- Can store strings and text values up to a maximum value of 2000 characters
VARCHAR2:- Can store strings and text values up to a maximum value of 4000 characters

If a column is defined as CHAR(10):- If the string entered is less than 10 characters, space characters
will be appended to the string, to meet the length of 10

If a column is defined as VARCHAR2(10):- If the string entered is less than 10 characters, no additional 
space characters will be appended to the string, to meet the length of 10
