VARCHAR2 also stores strings or text values, just like CHAR, with following differences
1.  Maximum length allowed is between 1 and 4000

The reason, its called VARCHAR2 and not CHAR, is because, even if you define the length of the element 
as VARCHAR2(20), but the string is only 5 characters long, the string will not be appended with 15 spaces 
at the end, unlike 'CHAR'
